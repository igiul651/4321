About Us

We believe that yummy sweets should be enjoyed by everyone, so we've created our own Vegan candy source of deliciousness.Vegan candy contains no ingredients derived from animals—no dairy, no gelatine, no honey, and no bone-char refined sugar(and we think that's pretty sweet). We are determined to bring you old classics and new tastes from around the world! With just the two of us here we are working on building our business in order to give back to animal rescue charities and to make plant-based confectionery the norm. We donate 5% of our profits to Brook Farm Animal Sanctuary. Brook FarmAnimal Sanctuary mainly rescues farmed animals, though it takes in any animal of any species in need of help.You can find more information about this amazing sanctuary by clicking here.We also believe that sustainability is important. We love our planet and all the creatures on it. And that’s why we’re working hard behind the scenes to use as much eco-friendly packaging as possible.

Description

Feast your eyes on our beautiful concoction of Vegan Pick &Mix. Fortunately, veganism is spreading so we know you now have lots of choices. Therefore, we have worked hard to bring you the best quality Vegan treats at a sweet price.

In your Pick &Mix selection you will receive a random assortment from the gallery below. We aim to give you a quality mix from our range, but unfortunately sometimes we will run out of certain candies (we're only earthlings after all).

We're frequently adding new varieties to our range, so keep checking for updates. We would love to hear from you with any suggestions for other candy you would like to see in the future.We hope you enjoy!
